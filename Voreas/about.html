Our Story

We are people who love good food and simplicity.

At Voréas, we serve food we would proudly share at our own table.

Everyday dishes, made with care, pure ingredients, and deep respect for Greek cuisine, just as we learned them, just as we love them.

Our Philosophy

Voréas was born out of our desire to create a space that feels authentic, without excess.

A meze restaurant, just the way we imagined it.

With flavors that smell like everyday Greece.

The name is no coincidence. Just as the North Wind clears the sky, we want to bring clear, honest flavor to the plate.
