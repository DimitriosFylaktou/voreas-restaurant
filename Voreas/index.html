Our Story

VOREAS is a mezedopoleio and mageireio in Athens, serving authentic Greek home-cooked dishes with respect for tradition and fresh, local ingredients. Every plate is a taste of Greek heritage and hospitality.

Whether you're a local or a traveler, VOREAS welcomes you with flavors that feel like home.
